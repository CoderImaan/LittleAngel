Our Story

18 Years of Nurturing Little Angels

Little Angel Pre Primary School was founded in 2005 by Mrs. Khan, a passionate early childhood educator with a dream to create a school where every child feels safe, loved, and excited to learn.

What began as a small playgroup of 12 children in a rented bungalow in Nigdi has today blossomed into a full-fledged pre-primary institution with 350+ students, 25 trained educators, and a purpose-built campus spread across 12,000 sq. ft.

Our school is registered under the Maharashtra Education Act and follows the National Curriculum Framework for Early Childhood Care and Education (NCF-ECCE) with an added layer of international best practices.

Principal's Message

A Message from Our Founder

"Every child who walks through our gates is a universe of potential. Our job is not to fill them with knowledge, but to light a fire of curiosity that burns for life."

Dear Parents and Guardians,

When I founded Little Angel in 2005, I had one simple belief: that the first years of a child's life are the most important — and they deserve the very best. Eighteen years later, that belief is stronger than ever.

At Little Angel, we don't just teach children the alphabet and numbers. We teach them to wonder, to question, to create, to collaborate, and most importantly, to be kind. We believe that social-emotional development is as critical as academic readiness.

Our dedicated team of educators, our thoughtfully designed curriculum, and our vibrant campus all work together to ensure your child leaves us not just school-ready, but life-ready.

I personally review our curriculum every year to incorporate the latest research in early childhood education. I invite you to visit us, see our school in action, and trust us with your most precious treasure.

With warm regards,
Mrs. Khan
