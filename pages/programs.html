Programs Designed for Every Stage

Our programs follow the NCF-ECCE 2022 guidelines, blending the best of Indian values with global early childhood education practices.

Nursery Program

The Nursery program is a child's first formal introduction to a learning environment. We focus on making this transition joyful, gentle, and stimulating. Children are introduced to routines, friendships, and self-expression through sensory play.

LKG – Lower Kindergarten

LKG builds upon the nursery foundation with structured learning. Children begin phonics, early numeracy, and are introduced to the wonders of science and nature. Our child-centred approach ensures curiosity drives every lesson.

UKG – Upper Kindergarten

UKG is the final and most exciting stage of pre-primary education. Children develop academic readiness while blossoming socially and emotionally. By the end of UKG, children confidently read simple sentences, write independently, and are fully prepared for Grade 1.

✅ Public speaking & drama
✅ Computer fundamentals

FAQs

Common Questions About Our Programs

Our primary medium of instruction is English, with Marathi as the second language. For Nursery children, we also accommodate Hindi-speaking families comfortably.

Yes! For LKG and UKG, mid-day meals are included. For Nursery (half-day), a healthy morning snack is provided. Our meals are prepared fresh daily in our on-campus kitchen.

Yes, GPS-tracked school transport is available for areas within 5 km of the school. Fees vary by route. A female attendant accompanies all trips. Please contact the office for route details.

We follow the NCF-ECCE 2022 (National Curriculum Framework for Early Childhood Care and Education) prescribed by the Government of India, enhanced with elements from the Montessori and Reggio Emilia approaches.

We use continuous, observation-based assessment (no written exams). Progress reports are shared quarterly. We conduct Parent-Teacher Meetings twice a year, plus an open house at year-end where children showcase their portfolios.
