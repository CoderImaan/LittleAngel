Our Hiring Standards

Quality Teaching Is Our Non-Negotiable

Every teacher at Little Angel goes through a rigorous 3-stage recruitment process. We verify qualifications, conduct background checks (police verification), and put every candidate through a live teaching demonstration with children present.
