Step by Step

Admission Process

Simple, transparent, and parent-friendly. From enquiry to first day – we guide you at every step.

1
Submit Online Enquiry or Call Us

Fill the form on this page or call +91 98765 432XX. Our admissions team will contact you within 4 hours on working days.

⏱️ Takes 5 minutes
2
Campus Tour & Interaction

Visit our campus with your child. See our classrooms, meet teachers, and let your child play in our campus. This informal interaction also helps us understand your child.

📅 By appointment only
3
Registration & Document Submission

Submit the registration form with required documents. Pay the non-refundable registration fee of ₹500 to confirm your slot.

4
Fee Payment & Confirmation

Pay the first-quarter tuition fee and admission fee. Receive your official admission confirmation letter and school kit date.

5
🎉 Welcome to Little Angel Family!

Attend Orientation Day, collect your school kit, uniform, and ID card. Your child's journey begins on the 1st day of the new session.

Be Prepared

  • 📋
    Previous school Transfer Certificate (for LKG/UKG transfers)

    Parent/Guardian Aadhaar card copies (2 nos.)
